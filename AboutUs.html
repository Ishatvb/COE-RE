Vision
To achieve excellence as a hub for exploring, educating, and advocating renewable energy, with the goal of providing the university community with the necessary knowledge to excel in research, education, and community engagement within the transformative realm of renewable energy and tools to play a leading role in shaping sustainable solutions.
Mission
  1. Pioneering global research, confronting energy challenges for sustainable solutions
  2. Cultivate deep understanding and equip students for renewable energy.
  3. Advocating policies, forging alliances, and engaging communities in sustainable energy missions.

22th to 28th February 2022 Celebration of National Science Week ("Azadi ka Amrit Mahotsava”)

Read more about this is some title

1st Day (Feb 22, 2022) - “Biography: Life of the great scientist”.

In the evening session on 22nd February society of Mechanical Engineering organized an event titled “Biography: Life of the great scientist”.

2nd Day (Feb 23, 2022) - VOLTRO

VOLTRO was a part of one of the various technical events organized under IETE Student‟s Forum (ISF) every year. The main objective was to make students more familiar with Arduino and basic Electronics. It was an open event for all students of the first and second year at JUET.

3rd Day (Feb 24, 2022) - TRIVIA

Civil engineering forum under the department of civil engineering, JUET, organized an event,“Trivia”. This event is based on the Quiz Competition with Visuals and General Science.

4th Day (Feb 25, 2022) - Kodeathon

On Friday, 25th February, a competitive coding contest: Kodeathon B1 – LITTLE SCIENTISTS was organized for the second year students by Bitwise and in association with CSI@JUET. Students were tasked to solve 5 problems in 2 hours.

5th Day (Feb 26, 2022) - Free Webinar/ Workshop on ROS

On 5th day, a session titled Robot Operating System has successfully organized on the theme of Robot meta-framework by Dr. Amit Kumar Srivastava, RoSPinoT section of AR/VR AI-ML and Robotics Development and Innovation Center.

Day 6th (Feb 28, 2022) - Workshop on Integrated approach in S&T for sustainable future.

National Science Week concluded with a workshop on sustainable S&T. Speakers highlighted green energy, renewable solutions for tribal communities, and achieving clean energy goals by 2070, including blockchain applications in renewables.

Day 6th (Feb 28, 2022) - Valedictory function

Valedictory session featured Chief Patron Prof. J.S.P. Rai and Patron Prof. Pramod Kumar Singh appreciating JUET's technical societies. Dr. Dhananjay R. Mishra reported on successful events, concluding with thanks from Dr. D. K. Shukla.
